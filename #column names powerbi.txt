#column names

SUBDIVISION YEAR JAN FEB MAR APR MAY JUN JUL AUG SEP OCT NOV DEC ANNUAL JF MAM JJAS OND

🌦️ Meaning of JF, MAM, JJAS, OND

IMD divides the year into four meteorological seasons:

1️⃣ JF = January + February (Winter Season)

It represents the total rainfall in Jan + Feb

IMD calls this Winter Rainfall

2️⃣ MAM = March + April + May (Pre-Monsoon Season)

Represents summer / pre-monsoon rainfall

Important for drought analysis and early agriculture patterns

3️⃣ JJAS = June + July + August + September (Southwest Monsoon Season)

This is the main monsoon period

70–80% of India’s annual rainfall happens in JJAS

Most important column for rainfall dashboards

4️⃣ OND = October + November + December (Post-Monsoon / Northeast Monsoon Season)

Significant for Tamil Nadu & South India

Called Northeast Monsoon Rainfall
